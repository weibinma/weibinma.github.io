About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at University of Delaware, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Lena Mashayekhy. Before entering PhD study in fall 2018, I received my B.S. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Dalian Maritime University, Dalian, China in 2014 and received my M.S. degree in Data Science at University at Buffalo, The State University of New York, Buffalo, USA in 2018.

Research Interests

Edge Computing, Computation/Video Offloading, Resource Allocation, Service Placement, Optimization.
